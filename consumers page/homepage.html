About Farmer's Market

The Farmer's Direct Market is an online platform where farmers can sell their products directly to consumers without the intervention of a middleman. The website aims to provide farmers with a reliable and sustainable source of income while giving the consumers access to fresh and affordable produce. Additionally, the website also features articles related to farming and a job board for hiring workers for specific farming related tasks..
